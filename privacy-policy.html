Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Noob Games ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with our game "Shadow of Time" ("Game").

1. Information We Collect

Shadow of Time is designed with your privacy in mind. We are committed to minimizing data collection to only what is necessary for gameplay functionality.

The game does not gather or process any personal data outside of your device except for:

  • Data required for multiplayer gaming functionality
  • Authentication information when logging in with Epic Games
  • Basic player activity metrics for multiplayer sessions

2. Epic Games Data Processing

When you use the Epic Games login functionality or participate in multiplayer gaming via the Epic Online Services (EOS), certain data is processed by Epic Games.

This data is governed by Epic Games' Privacy Policy. For information on how Epic Games collects and processes your data, please refer to:

Epic Games Privacy Policy

3. Data Storage

Game progress, settings, and configuration data are stored locally on your device. We do not store this information on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Game is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
