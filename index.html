About the Game

Shadow of Time is an intense multiplayer arena shooter where ancient civilizations collide. Choose your faction - command the mystical powers of Egyptian gods or unleash the brutal might of Viking warriors in fast-paced combat arenas.

Battle with friends online in various game modes, collect powerful weapons and temporary abilities through strategic powerup pickups. Login seamlessly with your existing gaming accounts through Epic Games integration.
