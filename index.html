About the Game

Shadow of Time is a story-driven indie adventure that lets you control the flow of time. Explore ancient ruins and futuristic cities as you solve puzzles that twist the past and future together.

Every decision echoes through history. Use your powers wisely to uncover secrets, forge alliances, and determine the fate of a world caught in a temporal storm.
